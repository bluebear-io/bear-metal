# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants in this project are expected to:

- Be respectful in technical disagreement
- Assume good intent, but challenge ideas rigorously
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and decisions rather than people
- Make space for contributors with different levels of experience

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing private information without permission
- Deliberate disruption, spam, or bad-faith engagement

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other maintainer-managed communication channels for bear-metal.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

For sensitive reports, do not open a public issue. Contact the maintainers through the repository owner or organization on GitHub. Include links, screenshots, and any other context needed to investigate the report.
